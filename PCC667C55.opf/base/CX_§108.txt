tadyathāpi nāma kāśyapa kuśalo aśvadamaka suto · yatra yatra pṛthivīpradeśe aśva skhalati ·
utkuṃbhati vā khaḍuṃkakkriyā vā karoti · tatra tatra caiva pṛthivīpradeśe nigṛhṇāti · sa tathā
tathā nigṛhṇāti yan na punar api na prakupyate · evam eva kāśyapa yogācāro bhikṣur yatra
yatraivaṃ cittasya vikāraṃ paśyati · tatra tatrraivāsya nigrahāya pratipadyate · sa tathā
tathā cittaṃ nigṛhṇāti yathā na puna prakupyate · tatredam ucyate · yathāśvasūta kuśalo
bhaveta skhalitaṃ ca aśvaṃ samabhigraheti ·yogī tathā cittavikāra dṛṣṭvā tathā nigṛhṇāti
yathā na kupyati ·
