tasmin tarhi kāśyapa iha mahāratnakūṭe dharmaparyāye śikṣitukāmena bodhisatvena yoniśo dharmaprayuktena
bhavitavyaṃ · tatra kāśyapa katamo yoniśa dharmaprayogaḥ yad uta sarvadharmāṇāṃ
bhūtapratyavekṣā · katamā ca kāśyapa sarvadharmāṇāṃ bhūtapratyavekṣā · yatra kāśyapa nātmapratyavekṣā
na satva na jīva na poṣa na pudgala na manuja na mānavapratyavekṣā · iyam ucyate
