atha khalu bhagavān punar evāyuṣmaṃtaṃ mahākāśyapam āmaṃtrayati sma · yasmiṃ kāśyapa deśe uṣṭradhūmaka
kṛṣṇaśira uttānaśāyī bhavati sa deśa sopadravaḥ sopakleśa sopāyāso bhavati · sacet
puna kāśyapa yasmiṃ deśe bodhisatvo bhavati · sa deśa nirupadrava nirupakleśa nirupāyāso
bhavati · tasmāt tarhi kāśyapa satvārthodyuktena bodhisatvena bhavitavyaṃ tena sarvakuśalamūlāni
sarvasatvānām utsraṣṭavyaṃ · sarvaṃ ca kuśalamūla samyak samudānayitavyaṃ · yac
ca jñānabheṣajyaṃ paryeṣate tena caturdiśaṃ gatvā sarvasatvānāṃ bhūtacikitsā kartavyā : bhūtacikitsāyāc
ca satvā cikitsitavyāḥ
