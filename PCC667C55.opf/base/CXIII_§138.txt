asmin khalu punar gāthābhinirhāre bhāṣyamāṇe aṣṭānāṃ bhikṣuśatānām anupādāyāsravebhyaś cittāni
vimuktāni · dvātriṃśatīnāṃ ca prāṇasahasrāṇāṃ virajo vigatamalaṃ dharmeṣu dharmacakṣur
viśuddhaṃ · paṃca bhikṣuśatāni dhyānalābhī utthāyāsanebhyaḥ prakkrāntāni imāṃ gaṃbhīrā dharmadeśanām
avataraṃto nāvagāhamānāḥ anadhimucyamānāḥ
